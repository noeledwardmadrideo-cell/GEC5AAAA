7 Cs of Communication

The 7 Cs are principles for effective communication:

Clarity: Easy to understand

Conciseness: Brief and to the point

Correctness: Grammatically accurate

Concreteness: Specific and detailed

Completeness: All necessary information

Courtesy: Respectful and polite

Coherence: Logical and organized
